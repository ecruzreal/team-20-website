About Healthy Wallet

Healthy Wallet is a financial planning tool for young adults, highlighting the importance of health savings accounts and financial health. A health savings account allows individuals to save, invest, and pay for medical expenses tax-free. Young adults may be hesitant or confused on why or how much to contribute, but Healthy Wallet makes it easy to learn with features ranging from peer forums, an AI chatbot, estimated/simulated costs, and educational information for users.

Features

Health Savings Account

On this dashboard, users may view their balance, contributions, limits, and learn more about the benefits of their health savings account from their financial institution

Community Forums

Users may browse through forums where others may have asked and answered similar questions. They may like and comment on posts. Additionally, users are able to ask questions by creating their own post, allowing others to view and answer their question.

AI Chatbot

Integrated AI tools allow users to analyze their financial goals and offer guidance through making financial decisions.

Calendar and Prescriptions

Medical visits, their reasons, and their costs can be tracked through the app’s calendar. Prescriptions can also be added, allowing for doses to be managed. This ensures that users are able to plan and budget so they will not miss a dose.
